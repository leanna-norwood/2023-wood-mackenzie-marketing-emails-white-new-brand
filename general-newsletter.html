Metals & Mining Snapshot: Focus on demand

  
 

Oil price down in 2020, but why?
 

This month's snapshot is a synthesis of the short-term outlooks which our teams released last week. The overriding theme was an improvement in sentiment as positive news came from China about capacity utilisation and most notably, government stimulus. This comes in sharp contrast to ex-China demand, which in many cases is not expected to reach 2019 levels again until 2022 or later.
